Company History

Dude, back in 2021, these two gnarly brothers, like, had a vision, man. They were shreddin' the waves in sunny Carlsbad, and after catching some sick barrels, they were craving the most bodacious, organic fruit smoothies ever, you know? So, they paddled out of the surf and founded their own juice and fresh fruit bar, bringing the freshest, most tubular smoothies straight to their fellow surfers and beachgoers. They're all about good vibes, healthy living, and like, keepin' it real with Mother Nature, man. Now, the whole community can ride the wave of delicious, organic fruit smoothies and snacks. Hang loose, and catch you on the flip side at our juice bar, bro!
