About Carlsbad

Carlsbad, California, is a picturesque coastal city located in North San Diego County. Known for its stunning beaches, world-class golf courses, and temperate climate, Carlsbad attracts visitors and residents alike who appreciate the laid-back Southern California lifestyle.

  • Carlsbad is home to LEGOLAND California.
  • The city has seven miles of coastline
  • It is home to several companies, including Callaway Golf, TaylorMade, and Viasat
  • Carlsbad is named after a famous spa in the Czech Republic, Karlsbad
