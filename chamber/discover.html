About Myrtle Creek

Myrtle Creek was founded in 1865 by John Hall and officially incorporated in 1913. It was named for the groves of Oregon Myrtle

  • Population: 3,535
  • Was once a major logging center
  • Near the beautiful South Umpqua Falls
  • Home to many historic buildings
