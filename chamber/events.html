About The Show & Shine Car Show

An annual tradition in Myrtle Creek where families and car lovers of all ages come and see the classic cars on display. This year was as good as any other with some great cars, food, and fun.

  • Where: Myrtle Park (Next to Tommy's)
  • What: The best car show ever.
  • When: July 3rd and 4th, 8:00-5:00
