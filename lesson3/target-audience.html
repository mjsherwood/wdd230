Target Audience:

The target audience are community members and small businesses owners who want to support local businesses and help the community and keep up with the local news.

Persona One: Small Business Owner

  • Fictional Name: Mike Linville
  • Job Title: Small Business Owner
  • Responsibilities: Hire employees, Maintain capitalization, Run business
  • Demographics: 25-60 years old, Family to support, Mid to Upper class
  • Goals/Tasks: Engage community, Make profit
  • Environment:

Persona One: Small Town Mom

  • Fictional Name: Melodie Sue
  • Job Title: Community member-Soccer Mom.
  • Responsibilities: Accountant, Housekeeper, At home teacher
  • Demographics: 25-45 years old, Family to support, Mid to Upper class
  • Goals/Tasks: Engage community, Support family,
  • Environment:

Scenarios:

  • Can I find a local businesses to cater my daughters wedding?
  • Is there a business that can help with my clogged drain?
  • Are there any new businesses in town?
  • Can I find any local businesses to sponsor the wrestling team?
  • Are there any of this type of business in town?
  • How can the Chamber of Commerce help support my business?
